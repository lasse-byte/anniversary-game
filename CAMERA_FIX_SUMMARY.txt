Detection Area:
  Size: 1100Ã—400 pixels
  Position (local): Vector2(500, 400)
  Position (global): Vector2(1100, 650)
  
  Why these values?
  - Width: 1100px (text box is 1000px + 50px margin each side)
  - Height: 400px (extends from text box to floor level)
  - X position: 500px (centers horizontally in text box)
  - Y position: 400px (places at floor where player walks)
  
  Player walks at y=650 (floor level)
  Text box at y=250, detection at 250+400=650 âœ“

Collision Settings:
  collision_layer = 0  (detection area not on any layer)
  collision_mask = 1   (detects layer 1 where player is)
  monitorable = false  (other objects don't detect it)

Camera Movement:
  - Uses existing player.gd camera system
  - Same as picture frames (enter_frame_view/exit_frame_view)
  - Smooth tween animations (1.5 seconds)
  - EASE_OUT + TRANS_CUBIC for natural motion
